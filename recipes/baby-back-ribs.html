Baby Back Ribs

Description

An easy recipe for top-tier tender ribs cooked on the grill!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 lbs baby back pork ribs
  • 1 cup barbeque sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat a gas grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Combine cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a small jar; close lid and shake to mix.
  3. Trim membrane sheath from back of each rack.
  4. Sprinkle rub onto both sides of the ribs as desired. Do not thrououghly rub.
  5. Place aluminum foil on lower rack to capture drippings and prevent flare-ups. Lay ribs on top rack of the grill, reduce gas heat to low and close the lid, leave for 1 hour.
  6. Brush ribs with barbeque sauce, and grill for 5 more minutes.
  7. Serve ribs as a rack or cut individually.
